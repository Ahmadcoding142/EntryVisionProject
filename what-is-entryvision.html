What is EntryVision?

The future of smart, a seamless facial recognition system.

How Does It Work?

EntryVision uses facial recognition technology to control who can enter. It connects directly to a smart door-lock system, so only people who are recognized and approved can unlock the door and go inside. .

AI Recognition

Our system continuously learns and improves, ensuring accurate and fast identification.

Secure Authentication

Using encrypted machine learning data, the door lock recognizes the trusted faces that are eligible to enter the door.

Immediate alert

EntryVision receives notifications when someones enters, exits, or when someone's face is not recongizable in a matter of minutes, keeping you up to date. .
